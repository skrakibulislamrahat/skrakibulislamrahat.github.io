Research Projects

Calibration Analysis for Referable Diabetic Retinopathy (IJBHI)

Multi-seed internal calibration analysis of a ResNet-18 fundus classifier trained on APTOS 2019 using Counterfactual Consistency Regularization. Reports ECE15, Brier score, reliability diagrams, and a constrained external diagnostic evaluation on Messidor-2 under a strict no-adaptation protocol.

Lightweight Diabetic Retinopathy Detection Models

Evaluation of MobileNetV2, EfficientNet-B0, and SqueezeNet with emphasis on accuracy–efficiency trade-offs for low-resource screening settings.

Multimodal Framework Research

Multimodal learning pipelines integrating medical images and structured clinical features to study robustness and interpretability.
